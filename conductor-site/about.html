About

The Conductor started with the Carnegie shop, where owner Matt saw a need for high-quality men's barbershops in the Bayside area. Highett soon followed, then Richmond — and he hasn't looked back.

One of Melbourne's leading barbershops, based in Richmond, Carnegie and Highett and servicing the surrounding areas. We pride ourselves on the best environment and service for every client, blending modern technique with the traditional barbershop feel so everyone feels at ease.
